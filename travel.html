Getting to Portland

Planes

Portland has an International Airport, making getting here from far away a breeze! Public transit services the airport, and Portland has several rideshare options.

Trains

Take the train and enjoy the ride. No matter which direction you come from, the route is beautiful. Coordinate with friends to take the trip together!

Automobiles

If you live close by you may choose to take a bus, carpool with locals, or drive yourself. This is usually the quickest (and most flexible) option.
